Our Story

From Passion to Perfection

ELLIE HOMES is a Nigerian luxury interior design brand founded and led by ELLIE HOMES in Gwarimpa, Abuja. With a passion for elegant, modern spaces, Elle started the business from her home studio with a commitment to impeccable craftsmanship and exceptional service.

From the very first client, Elle set a standard of personalized design, refined fabrics, and tailored layouts. Her mission is to make high-end interior experiences accessible and relevant to Nigerian homes and businesses.

Each project is personally overseen by ELLIE HOMES, ensuring every curtain, room, and detail reflects her vision for contemporary luxury with local heart.
